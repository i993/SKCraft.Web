Rules

These rules are not exhaustive; please follow the spirit of the rules.

  • Be respectful to other users.
  • Don't be disruptive. Continue disagreements in private chat.
  • Don't use SKCraft as a place to advertise.
  • Refrain from excess vulgar language.
  • Sexually explicit content is prohibited.
  • Using addons or other tools to gain an unfair advantage is not allowed. Minimaps are acceptable.
  • Do not exploit bugs. Report them by contacting staff via private messages.
  • Don't flood or fill chat with spam.
  • You are always responsible for what happens on your account, regardless of who uses it.


No Theft on PvE Servers

Most of our servers are also PvE (player versus entity), so you may not damage other users' property or steal from them.

Suspensions

Your SKCraft account might get suspended if you violate (or repeatedly violate) one of our rules. In such an incident, you can ask for more information or request a ban appeal by contacting us on the forums (forum account required). Please provide some details when you send your message.
